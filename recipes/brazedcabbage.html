Brazed Cabbage

An easy, healthy, and tasty side.

Ingredients

  • One head of Cabbage
  • Six tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 farenheit
  2. Quarter the Cabbage
  3. Braze the cabbage
  4. Sprinkle cabbage with salt & pepper.
  5. Place cabbage quarters on baking sheet
  6. Bake until cabbae is tender
