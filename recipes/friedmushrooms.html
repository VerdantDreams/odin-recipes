Panko Fried Mushrooms

A fried mushroom recipe sans egg.

Ingredients

  • One basket of Whole Baby Bella Mushrooms
  • One cup of Flour
  • One tablespoon of Franks Red Hot sauce
  • One tablespoon of Nutritional Yeast
  • One tablespoon of Garlic Powder
  • One tablespoon of Onion Powder
  • One tablespoon of Celery Salt
  • One quarter cup of Soy Sauce
  • Two cups of Panko Crumbs
  • Water as necessary

Steps

  1. Add the Flour, Nutritional Yeast, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Celery Salt, Franks Red Hot sauce, & Soy Sauce together in a large bowl.
  2. Stir water into bowl of ingredients until the mixture has the consistency of pancake mix.
  3. Place panko into a seperate bowl.
  4. Dip mushrooms one at a time into the batter, being sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Place mushrooms individualy into the panko bowl and coat the batterd mushrooms.
  6. Fill frying pan to a quarter full with vegitable oil, and preheat it. You'll know it's hot enough when the oil sputters after you place a drop of water into the pan.
  7. Place mushrooms in pan in an even layer.
  8. Flip the mushrooms when the bottom is a golden brown.
  9. When both the top and the bottom are golden brown, remove the mushrooms from the pan and place on a plate with paper towels, or a drying rack, and allow them to cool.
  10. Plate and enjoy with your choice of dipping sauce, or plain.
