Taco TVP

A taco meat substitute that's pretty close to beef.

Ingredients

  • One tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • One tablespoon Onion Powder
  • One tablespoon Cumin Powder
  • One quarter cup of Soy Sauce
  • One cup of Water
  • One quarter cup of Textured Vegitable Protien

Steps

  1. Add the dry ingredients, soy sauce, and a quarter cup of water to the pan.
  2. Turn on the burner and stir constantly
  3. Add water when the pan is dry until the TVP is soft
